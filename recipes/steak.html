Steak Recipe

Description

This simple yet delicious steak recipe will help you achieve a perfectly juicy and flavorful steak with a crispy seared crust.

Ingredients

  • Steak (ribeye, sirloin, or filet mignon)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a pan with olive oil until very hot.
  3. Sear the steak for a few minutes on each side.
  4. Add butter, garlic, and rosemary to the pan.
  5. Baste the steak with butter until cooked to desired doneness.
  6. Let it rest before slicing and serving.

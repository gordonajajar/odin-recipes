Omurice Recipe

Description

Omurice is a popular Japanese fusion dish that combines fried rice with a fluffy omelette on top, often served with ketchup or demi-glace sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Ketchup
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© chicken, onion, and carrot.
  2. Add cooked rice and mix with ketchup.
  3. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook a thin omelette.
  5. Place the omelette over the rice.
  6. Drizzle with ketchup and serve.
